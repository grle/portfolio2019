02. CURRICULUM DESIGN

I was given a general curriculum, which included grammar and reading exercises. However, I believed that the course would benefit if there was some unifying structure binding the books together, so I created my own curriculum in which I discussed the books in relation to the following themes:

  1. Death and trauma
  2. Healing and forgiveness
  3. Storytelling
  4. Motherhood vs fatherhood
  5. Role of race and gender
  6. Religion and faith

03. POWERPOINT
